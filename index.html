II. About Me

Hello! My name is Scott. I began my programming journey back in 2018 with HTML & CSS. That first line of code -- despite it being nothing more than a simple paragraph in HTML -- was all it took. I instantly knew this is what I was meant to do.

Since then, I've spent over 10,000 hours improving my skills. Yes I've kept track.

Finally, at the end of 2021 I knew I had what it took to be a major asset to any company willing to give me my first opportunity. In January of 2022 that opportunity came with Caesars Sportsbook, which is where I currently work.

I mainly build internal tools for sports traders and execs to use. Data analysis and Excel automation are a couple of my other regular tasks.

Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:
